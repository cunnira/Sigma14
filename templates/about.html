Cunningham Digital Solutions develops intelligent workflow automation platforms that help organizations improve operational efficiency, maintain compliance, and confidently manage mission-critical business processes.

Our solutions are designed for industries where reliability, security, traceability, and precision are essential—not optional. By combining modern cloud technologies with practical artificial intelligence, we build software that streamlines complex workflows while keeping people firmly in control of important decisions.

The company was founded on more than two decades of experience designing, implementing, and auditing systems within highly regulated industrial environments. That background continues to shape every solution we build today—from healthcare applications to industrial operations platforms.

We believe artificial intelligence delivers the greatest value when it works alongside people rather than replacing them. Every solution is designed to reduce administrative burden, improve decision making, eliminate repetitive work, and provide complete visibility into critical business processes.

What We Deliver

  • AI-Enabled Workflow Automation – Intelligent platforms that automate business processes while supporting informed human decision making.
  • Regulated Industry Solutions – Secure, compliant applications for organizations operating in healthcare, energy, manufacturing, and other regulated environments.
  • Cloud-Native SaaS Platforms – Modern multi-tenant applications built to scale with enterprise growth while maintaining high availability and security.
  • Operational Intelligence – Embedded AI assistants that help employees locate information, answer questions, summarize documentation, and increase productivity.
  • Mission-Critical Software Engineering – Systems designed with enterprise-grade reliability, auditability, and resilience at their core.

Our Mission

We help organizations replace outdated, disconnected processes with intelligent software that improves efficiency, strengthens compliance, and empowers employees to do their best work. Whether supporting patient engagement, industrial safety, or future workflow solutions, our goal is the same: deliver technology that becomes a strategic advantage.
