Why choose SigmaCare Connect?

SigmaCare Connect brings patients, providers, and administrative teams into one secure cloud-based experience. It reduces paperwork, improves patient satisfaction, and makes clinical workflows easier to manage across every visit.

Secure patient access

Provide secure online access to forms, appointments, messages, and clinical documents from any device.

Better communication

Keep patients informed and engaged with secure messaging, notifications, and centralized documentation.

Streamlined intake

Move registrations, demographic updates, insurance capture, and consent forms online to reduce manual intake work.

Cloud-based platform

Hosted securely in the cloud with automatic updates, backups, and enterprise-grade availability.

Core capabilities

The Patient Portal supports the full patient journey, from registration and intake to care coordination, education, and follow-up.

Patient intake and registration

  • Digital intake forms and customizable questionnaires.
  • Capture demographics, insurance, and consent information online.
  • Upload IDs, insurance cards, and supporting documents securely.

Secure communication

  • Encrypted messaging between patients, providers, and office staff.
  • Notifications for appointment reminders, instructions, and follow-ups.
  • Centralized access to visit summaries, education, and care plans.

Document and workflow management

  • Organize patient documents, intake paperwork, and clinical records.
  • Streamline provider workflows and reduce administrative handoffs.
  • Support specialty-specific processes with configurable workflows.

Analytics and growth readiness

  • Track portal adoption, patient engagement, and operational efficiency.
  • Prepare for future integrations with practice management and billing systems.
  • Enable future AI-assisted automation and intelligent document processing.
