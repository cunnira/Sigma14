SigmaShield Isolation

Modern energy isolation management for industrial operations that need safer, more efficient work execution, stronger compliance visibility, and a connected workflow for frontline teams.

Why choose SigmaShield Isolation?

SigmaShield Isolation is built for facilities managers, safety leaders, and operations teams who want to replace paper-based procedures with a cloud-native application designed for real-world energy isolation work. It helps you reduce risk, improve audit readiness, and keep teams aligned across multiple sites.

Safer operations

Track every energy source, breaker, valve, and lock with clear procedural steps, timestamps, and accountability built into every workflow.

Stronger compliance

Capture audit-ready records, detailed procedure histories, and access logs so you can demonstrate OSHA, ANSI, and internal compliance when it matters most.

Digital visibility

Move from spreadsheets to a centralized platform where energy isolation status is visible across teams, locations, and shifts.

Flexible workflows

Configure procedures to reflect your facility’s equipment, energy types, and safety protocols without forcing teams into one-size-fits-all steps.

What the platform offers

SigmaShield Isolation is designed to support the full life cycle of energy isolation work, from system cataloging and procedure setup to final release and reporting.

Energy source and device management

  • Catalog electrical, hydraulic, pneumatic, mechanical, and other energy sources.
  • Track locks, valves, breakers, tags, and isolation devices with unique identifiers.
  • Map energy sources to facility locations, equipment, and work orders.

Structured isolation workflows

  • Step-by-step isolation tracking with status updates and approval handoffs.
  • Bleeder and drain point management for safe energy dissipation.
  • Collaborative task assignment by role, location, or team.

Multi-site operations

  • Manage multiple facilities, buildings, units, and tenant organizations in a single platform.
  • Standardize processes while allowing site-level customization where needed.

Reporting and audit readiness

  • Export procedure records and audit logs for inspections, safety reviews, and compliance teams.
  • Preserve a complete history of actions, approvals, and device state changes.
  • Generate reports quickly for OSHA, internal safety audits, or regulatory review.

User and access controls

  • Role-based access for administrators, operators, and observers.
  • Granular permissions per facility, procedure, and energy source.
  • Track user activity for accountability and incident investigation.
